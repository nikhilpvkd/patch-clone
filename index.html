  • what is rent tracking

    rent tracking is a new way of boosting your credit history we track your rent payments to your landloards or agent. if you pay on time, we inform this to credit reference agency experian and they add this information to your credit report.

  • how does rent tracking work

    it's very easy.

    • sign up to patch
    • connect your bank account
    • pay your rent
    • patch report your payments to experian
    • your credit history improves

  • how do i see what you are tracked?

    you will start receiving the benifits of rent tracking as soon as you sign up. you won't actually able to view your inforamtion in your experian credit report until around three months after sign up.

  • how much does rent tracking cost?

    our rent tracking services is free to use.

  • hoe does rent tracking improve my credit history?

    your credit history improves each time a new, positive record is added to yourcredit report such as making a payment on time.your rent is now qualifying payment.

  • what if i change my property?

    not a problem. it is easy to continue rent tracking. log in to your patch account after you make your last payment to your old property and let us know about your new place...
